About the Office

AFSAAD Law Offices

AFSAAD Law Offices is an established law firm serving clients in Lebanon and internationally, with a legacy dating back to 1929.

The office is presented publicly as carrying a family legal legacy through Abdul Aziz Saad (Sr.), Dr. Farouk Saad, and Abdul Aziz Saad (Jr.). It focuses on Lebanese and international legal services with an emphasis on precision, integrity, and practical solutions.
